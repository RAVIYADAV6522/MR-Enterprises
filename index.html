About MR Enterprises

Established in 2026, MR Enterprises is an emerging supplier of natural and eco-friendly cow dung products, committed to serving the growing needs of organic farming and sustainable agriculture.

Working closely with reliable dairy farms and rural suppliers across Rajasthan, we ensure consistent quality and timely delivery to customers across India. Our products are 100% natural, sun-dried, and completely chemical-free.

Our mission is to support organic farming and provide natural alternatives that benefit both the environment and agriculture — while empowering rural communities along the way.

Our Products

All products sourced from healthy desi cows. Available in bulk for farms, nurseries, religious institutions, and industrial use.

Dry Cow Dung Cakes

Naturally sun-dried cow dung cakes — our primary product. Ideal for fuel, rituals, and organic farming applications.

  • Havan, yajna & religious ceremonies
  • Organic farming & soil enrichment
  • Vermicompost preparation
  • Clean fuel applications

Cow Dung Powder / Manure

Finely processed gobar powder — a powerful organic manure that naturally enriches soil and boosts crop yield.

  • Organic fertilizer for crops
  • Home garden & nursery use
  • Vermicompost raw material
  • Soil conditioning

Raw Cow Dung (Gobar)

Fresh gobar for plastering, natural floor coating, biogas production, and direct fertilizer application.

  • Traditional floor & wall plastering
  • Biogas production
  • Direct soil application
  • Industrial composting
