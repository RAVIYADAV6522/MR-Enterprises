About Us

From Nature to Nourishment

MR Enterprises is a trusted supplier of premium-quality dry cow dung cakes and organic cow dung products. We provide eco-friendly and naturally sun-dried products for agriculture, gardening, vermicomposting, religious purposes, and industrial applications.

Established in 2026, MR Enterprises is an emerging supplier of natural and eco-friendly cow dung products, committed to serving the growing needs of organic farming and sustainable agriculture. Working closely with reliable dairy farms and rural suppliers, we ensure consistent quality and timely delivery to customers across India.

Our mission is to support organic farming and provide natural alternatives that benefit both the environment and agriculture.
